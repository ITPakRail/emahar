Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, including your Name, Business Name, Email Address, and Phone Number. We also store the data you input into your invoices (Client details and Item lists) within your private database.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the invoicing services of eInvoicer. This includes generating PDFs, managing client ledgers, and maintaining your business settings. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Cloud infrastructure. Each vendor is assigned a unique, private database to ensure multi-tenant isolation and security.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your information at any time through your dashboard settings. For complete account deletion, please contact support@emahar.com.
