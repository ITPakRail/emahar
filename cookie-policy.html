Cookie & Storage Policy

eMahar.com uses cookies and local browser storage to improve your experience. Here is how we use them:

Type Purpose Duration
Authentication Stores your session data (User ID and Business Name) so you don't have to log in every time. Until Logout
Preferences Saves your language (English/Urdu) and sidebar toggle state. Persistent

How to Control Cookies

You can clear your browser's "Local Storage" and "Cookies" at any time via your browser settings. However, doing so will require you to log in again on your next visit.
